About CA&ES

The College of Agricultural & Environmental Sciences is dedicated to solving real-world problems in the agricultural, environmental, and human sciences to produce a better world, healthier lives, and an improved standard of living for everyone.

Established in 1922, the College encompasses 29 undergraduate majors and 45 graduate groups and programs.
