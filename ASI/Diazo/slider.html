Solution Centers

SAREP Solution Centers use new communication and engagement methods to provide information for decision support to farmers, ranchers and communities across California, and a variety of outreach partners, including UC advisors and specialists.

These virtual and in-person engagement efforts are meant to increase the accessibility of university research and create opportunities for farmers and researchers to engage in discussion around specific topics pertinent to California agriculture. Learn more about the Solution Center from our concept note.

Solution Center for Nutrient and Irrigation Management

SAREP is currently piloting its first Solution Center, the Solution Center for Nutrient and Irrigation Management, to help growers and agriculture professionals have better access to information and resources pertaining to nutrient management.

In recent years, nutrient management (particularly nitrogen) has become an increasingly important issue in California. Nutrient management plays an essential role in ensuring the healthy growth of agricultural crops, but can be a source of environmental contamination when used in excess. Balancing the needs of crops with the protection of California’s people and environment has become a major priority in our state’s agriculture.

The information farmers and agriculture professionals need to meet changing nutrient management requirements is often disparate and difficult to access. The Solution Center for Nutrient and Irrigation Management will be an information resource on nutrient management and will provide opportunities to engage with researchers and farmers on pressing nutrient management needs.

These virtual and in-person engagement efforts are meant to increase the accessibility of university research and create opportunities for farmers and researchers to engage in discussion around specific topics pertinent to California agriculture.
