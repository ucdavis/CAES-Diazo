Enhancing urban living through horticulture

Cutting-edge Horticultural Information for Californians

Located at the University of California, Davis, CCUH draws upon the knowledge and expertise of our partners in academia, industry, and the public with a mission to help Californians:

  • Develop more water-conserving, pest-resistant and disease-resistant home gardens.
  • Create environmentally sound public landscapes and parks.
  • Produce better plant materials for sustainable urban landscapes.

Addressing our fast-growing state's

  • increasing residential water demands
  • loss of wild lands
  • rising levels of harmful chemical inputs into the environment.

Improving the quality of urban life through:

  • PUBLIC PROGRAMS
  • DEMONSTRATION GARDENS
  • RESEARCH
