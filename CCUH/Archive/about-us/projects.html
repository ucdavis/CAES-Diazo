ADVANCING California's Nursery Industry & Public Horticultural Education

  • UC Davis Arboretum All-Stars Commercial Plant Introduction

Bringing California native plants and other heat- and drought-tolerant species from the UC Davis Arboretum's collection to California nurseries and home gardeners. More here.

  • Helping the Honey Bees

Partnering with Haagen-Dazs and the UC Davis Department of Entomology to support the honey bee populations through  educational outreach. More here.

  • ‘UC Verde’ ® Buffalograss

Promoting the use of hardy, water-conserving turf grass for lawns and landscapes. More here.

 

PARTNERING with Research for Water Quality and Conservation

  • Plant Trials Program for Sustainable Landscape Plants

Evaluating the performance of the UC Davis Arboretum All-Stars at various irrigation levels, climate zones and soil types.  More here.

  • Urban Run-off Water Quality Research

Characterizing neighborhood run-off water, educating gardeners on their role in reducing pollutants in natural waterways, and producing models for urban planning.  More here.

  • Water Conservation Publications and Links

An industry dialog to address the future of landscape water use and California's Model Water Efficient Landscape Ordinance. More here.
