Demonstration Landscape Open House

Discover Gardening Practices that Protect our Water!
Click image for flier

On October 2, the University of California's South Coast Research & Extension Center in Irvine will be opening its doors to the public to showcase its premier Demonstration Landscape designed especially to show you ways to reduce water and chemical run-off from your own property.

Booths and Informal Talks throughout the day on:

  • Irrigation
    • Composting
      • Low-water Use Plants
    • Pest Management
  • Run-Off Prevention

UCCE Staff and Master Gardeners will be on hand to answer questions.
