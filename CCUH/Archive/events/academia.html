Academic Partnerships

 

BRINGING RESEARCH FINDINGS to TARGET AUDIENCES in REAL TIME:

  • Providing a conduit to industry and the public for horticultural research results
  • Making environmental research accessible to the public and agencies it affects most

  • Helping the Honey Bees

Partnering with Häagen-Dazs and the UC Davis Department of Entomology to support the honey

bee populations through a demonstration garden contest and educational outreach. More here.

  • Plant Trials Program for sustainable landscape plants

Laying the groundwork for bringing California native plants and other heat- and drought-tolerant species from the UC Davis Arboretum's collection to California nurseries and home gardeners. More here.

  • Urban Run-off Water Quality Research

Characterizing neighborhood run-off water, educating gardeners on their role in reducing pollutants in natural waterways, and producing models for urban planning. More here.
